Crowdfunding for industry outsiders

Peak Agency runs fundraising campaigns with industry outsiders. Our mission is to get radical political initiatives both fundable and funded.

We succeed by realizing shared narratives and activating community power. We are a new collective working in Oakland, CA for economic justice. Our experience covers cooperative nonprofit development, community and online organizing, and campaign strategy. Services scale from small fundraising workshops and training sessions through to full campaign direction and production.
